Host a rig — become a node

Keep your GPU in your own home. We route only open, non-PHI work to it — a patient record can never reach it (the firewall refuses it at the boundary). Your rig earns for the people.

1 · Clone + run the worker

git clone https://github.com/SudoSuOps/OpenDiabetic-Compute.git
cd OpenDiabetic-Compute
python3 odc_worker.py --hive http://YOUR-HIVE:8770 --token <your-token> \
  --node my-rig --owner "Your Name" --gpu "RTX 5090" --vram 32 \
  --ollama-model llama3.2:3b --interval 20

2 · Your rig registers, leases jobs, returns receipts

It heartbeats, leases NON-PHI jobs, runs them on your local model, and reports a hash-chained receipt — fully visible on the dashboard.
